About

I'm Veronica, a student, fitness enthusiast and runner. I wasn't born a runner or an athlete in high school, I was the girl in the gym class walking the mile run. At age 21, I started going to the gym, mainly for aesthetic reasons. Some years after I became very interested in health and fitness, I was focusing on becoming the healthiest version of myself. During the pandemic, when gyms closed, I did what a lot of people did and started running. I went from not even being able to run half a mile, despite having downloaded couch to 5k a decade ago. Somehow, I persevered and ran my first half marathon. Then I back tracked and ran my first 10k then 5k (I know, its backwards). Since then, I have done a handful of races and became a certified nutritionist through NASM.
